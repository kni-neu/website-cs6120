# Karl Ni

Karl Ni is the lead instructor for CS 6120: Natural Language Processing. He is a Researcher and Software Engineer with extensive experience in machine learning and scalable systems.

## Research Interests

His work focuses on the intersection of linguistic theory, deep learning scale, and agentic workflows. He is particularly interested in how we can build more reliable and capable AI systems through a combination of first-principles engineering and modern modeling techniques.

## Background

Karl has a background in both academia and industry, having worked on some of the world's most advanced NLP systems. He is passionate about teaching and helping students bridge the gap between theoretical concepts and practical, production-ready applications.
