# Final Project Hub: Creative NLP Application

## Overview
The final project is an opportunity to dive deep into a specific NLP problem and build a functional, end-to-end application. You will work in teams of 3-4 to define, implement, and evaluate a creative NLP solution.

## Important Dates
- **Project Proposal:** Week 10
- **Final Implementation:** Week 12
- **Final Report & Presentation:** Finals Week

## Project Requirements
Your project must satisfy the following criteria:
1. **Model Complexity:** Leverage modern techniques (LLMs, RAG, or fine-tuned Transformer models).
2. **Implementation:** Provide a clean, modular repository with clear setup instructions.
3. **Application:** The project must have a functional interface (web app, CLI tool, or API).
4. **Evaluation:** Quantitative and qualitative analysis of your model's performance.

## Submission
All materials must be submitted via the course Canvas portal.
